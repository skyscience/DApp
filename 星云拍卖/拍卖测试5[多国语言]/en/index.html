Install/use wallet

First download the plugin →Click me to download
After the download is complete, unzip it to a folder. Open extensions in Google Chrome

Select Extract to Folder.

The plug-in icon is displayed after the installation is complete.

Open the plug-in and click New Wallet, set the password (password is at least 9 digits) and click Create New Wallet.

Special reminder: Please be sure to ensure the security of the key file, do not forget the password (the private key is the certificate of your digital assets, please do not tell anyone, if you accidentally exposed or lost, you will face the risk of losing the corresponding digital assets, please be cautious ).


Click to download the password database file and save it to your computer. This contains the private key information in this wallet. Please keep it safe and avoid loss or theft.

Note: The password library file can be used to disconnect the network and conduct offline transactions. The key information in this wallet is included. Please keep it properly. If you accidentally lose it, you will face the risk of losing the corresponding digital assets.


After the completion, you have successfully established a NAS wallet!

Sale items

Click Auction Upload Auction


Fill in the item contents and auction duration as shown in the image and click the Start Auction button.

Take items

Find unfinished auction items in the auction list, click to participate in the auction button

Enter your bid at the bottom of the item details page and click the Participate Auction button!

Confirm that the auction has been completed

First confirm that the auction is confirmed by the user with the highest bid price after the auction time, and the user’s funds that were previously bid will be returned to the account.

Click to confirm the completion of the auction button to complete the auction!
